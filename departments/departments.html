Artisan in Electrical Installation

This Course is designed specifically for primary 8 school leavers in line with the national objectives for training programs. The course emphasis is on developing practical skills that will enable the graduate to carry out domestic installation and repair basic appliances correctly and safely. He/she will also be able to carry out the wiring of light industrial installations.

ENTRY REQUIREMENT

Electrical Wireman Trade Test

The Electrical Wireman skill is developed to test competencies of persons engaged in the Electrical Industry in order to standardize their operation and certify them the suitability to put tasks expected at the level. Persons at this level of competence have useful skills which are required for the performance of routine tasks applicable in the trade.

Solar Photovoltaic Installation

The Solar Photovoltaic Course is developed to test the competencies of persons engaged in the solar photovoltaic systems design and installation work for small to medium single battery direct current systems, and multiple batteries which may include an inverter (incorporating single or multiple modules up to 100-300 Wp). This is in order to standardize their operations and certify them for their sustainability to carry out tasks expected at this level.

A Minimum KCPE
